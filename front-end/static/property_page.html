Property Description

This Spanish retreat is located only 5 kilometers from the center of Valencia. Located near the beach, you can enjoy the hot sun while swimming in the beautiful blue ocean. The property is also located near many restaurants, enjoy Spanish cuisine such as Tapas and many more. Valencia is a city known for its architecture, culture, and rich history. There is an endless amount of things that you can do there, sightseeing, eating, walking, going to the beach, the possibilities are endless. The property itself has Moorish influence and has the design of a mosque's interior as a homage to Spain's Islamic past. The property comes with 1 bedroom and 1 bathroom and is suitable for 2 guests, perfect for a couple's honeymoon!
